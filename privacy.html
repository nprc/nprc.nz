Privacy Policy

We do not collect, store or sell your personal information.

In addition to our website, the New Plymouth Reformed Church has several apps for your free use on the Android, Windows, and Windows Phone app stores. For these applications, neither the New Plymouth Reformed Church, nor their developers Conglomo collect, store or sell your personal information. Please see our Github repository to view the source code of our apps and website, and investigate for yourself.

Any support enquiries for the website or apps should be directed to webmaster@nprc.nz.
