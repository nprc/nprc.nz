Reformation Principles

As church we hold to and subscribe to the five great fundamental rallying cries of the Protestant Reformers.

The five great rally cries of the Protestant Reformation, commonly called “the solas”, are:

  1. “Sola Scriptura” (Scripture Alone)
  2. “Sola Gracia” (Grace Alone)
  3. “Sola Fide” (Faith Alone)
  4. “Solas Christus” (Christ Alone)
  5. “Sola Deo Gloria” (To God be Alone Glory)

The Reformed Churches of New Zealand use the following four confessions as basis of our systematic theology:

  1. The Belgic Confession
  2. The Heidelberg Catechism
  3. The Canons of Dort
  4. The Westminster Confession
